What happens next?

  • OCR or document parsing extracts report text.
  • Plain summary is generated in simple words.
  • Urgency and trust score are calculated.
  • MediVerify checks values against ranges.
  • MediBot answers questions and builds medicine reminders.

For image OCR, make sure Tesseract language data is available on your machine. Typed text and document parsing work best when the report is clearly formatted.
